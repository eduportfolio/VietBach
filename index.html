Research & Personal Product

Research Publication

International Journal of Scientific Research and Management (IJSRM) • 2025

Applications and Impacts of IoT in Vietnam's Logistics Industry

Abstract

Vietnam's logistics ecosystem has rapidly developed, with the country listed among top Asian nations in the field. High technologies such as AI, Big Data, Cloud Computing, and Internet of Things (IoT) have been applied to support fast development with reduced risks. This paper presents the applications and impacts of IoT for Vietnam's logistics sector, emphasizing its critical role through numerical analysis and providing recommendations for future development.

Key Contributions
  • IoT integration in real-time tracking & warehouse management
  • 20-30% improvement in inventory accuracy
  • Adoption barriers and infrastructure analysis
  • Arduino-based prototype development
Research Impact
  • Predictive maintenance cost reduction
  • Enhanced supply chain transparency
  • SME digital transformation roadmap
